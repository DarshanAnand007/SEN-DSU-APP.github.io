Reporting a Vulnerability
We take the security of the sen_app_latest project seriously. If you discover a vulnerability, we encourage you to report it as soon as possible to help us maintain the safety and security of our users.

How to Report
Email: Please send an email to [your security contact email] with the details of the vulnerability. Include a detailed description of the vulnerability and steps to reproduce it.

Response Time: You can expect a response within 72 hours of your report. We will work with you to understand the issue and take appropriate action.

Updates: We will provide you with regular updates on the status of the vulnerability you reported. You will be notified when the vulnerability has been resolved or if we require additional information.

Resolution: If the vulnerability is accepted, we will work on a fix and apply the necessary updates to the affected versions. You will be informed once the fix is available and any relevant patches have been released.

Confidentiality
Please keep any details regarding the vulnerability confidential until we have confirmed a resolution and released a patch. Publicly disclosing the vulnerability before this could put our users at risk.

Thank You
We appreciate your efforts to keep sen_app_latest safe and secure for everyone. Your contributions to the security of our project are highly valued.
